How CareAlert Works

Our intelligent system follows a streamlined process to ensure optimal patient monitoring and timely interventions.

1

Data Collection

IoT sensors continuously collect patient vital signs including heart rate, temperature, and blood pressure in real-time.

2

Data Processing

ESP32 microcontroller processes raw sensor data, filters noise, and prepares it for transmission to the cloud server.

3

Data Transmission

Processed data is transmitted via WiFi to the cloud server for real-time monitoring and analysis.

4

Dashboard Updates

Real-time updates on dashboard with AI-powered risk assessment and alerts for medical staff.

About This Project

CareAlert is a comprehensive project that demonstrates the integration of IoT technology, artificial intelligence, and modern web development to solve real-world healthcare challenges. This intelligent ICU monitoring system goes beyond traditional dashboards by incorporating predictive analytics, early warning capabilities, and automated reporting features.

The system is designed to assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions through real-time monitoring, trend analysis. With its intuitive interface and advanced features, CareAlert represents the future of intensive care unit patient monitoring.
